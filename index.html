Our Story
Born on the Coast.
Built for the Wild.

Mike couldn't find a marine wash that actually worked. As a qualified auto tech with years on the water, he knew there had to be something better.

So he built it. Collaborating with "Prof. Paul" and a team of industry experts, they ran over 100 split tests to create the most advanced marine and off-road wash on the market — one that strips 100% of salt in seconds while staying kind to the environment.

That was the start of Salt Shift. An Australian company dedicated to protecting the gear that gets you out there.
